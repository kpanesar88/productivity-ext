Frequently Asked Questions

Do I always need a tab running?

No, you don't always need to keep a tab running. Our extension is designed to work in the background and will notify you when it needs your attention.

Is the camera always watching me?

No, the camera is not always watching you. It is only activated when you explicitly use features that require it, and you will be notified beforehand.

Do I have to install it?

Yes, to use our service, you need to install the browser extension. The extension enables additional features and functionalities that are essential for the optimal performance of our service. Installation is quick and straightforward, and detailed instructions are provided to guide you through the process.
